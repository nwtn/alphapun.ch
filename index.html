About

What is this?

An image with a transparent background is like a painting on a window: you can see all sorts of exciting things behind it, but you can’t reach through to touch them. alphaPun.ch changes that!

alphaPun.ch will trace the opaque part of your PNG or GIF image. It will then punch through the alpha channel (i.e. the transparent bit), so you can click on things behind it.

What’s really going on?

alphaPun.ch creates a mask for the opaque part of the image, along with masks for the clickable content behind it. These masks are placed above the actual image. Clicking a link’s mask will trigger a click on that link.

A detailed technical explanation can be found in Introducing alphaPun.ch.
